# Crew Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-27_

Crew ("we", "the app") is a live group‑ride tracking app for riders. This policy
explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices. Please review it with
your own legal counsel before publishing — this is a starting template, not legal advice.

## Information we collect

- **Account info:** email address, display name, rider tag (username), and optional
  bike description, provided when you sign up (via Apple, Google, or email).
- **Location:** your device's GPS location, collected **only while you turn on
  location sharing**. With your permission, this may include background location so
  your crew can still see you during navigation or with the screen locked.
- **Voice messages:** short audio clips you record and send to your crew.
- **Social graph:** the friends you add and the ride rooms you join.

## How we use your information

- To show your location on the map to people you have chosen to share with —
  your accepted friends and members of a ride room you have joined.
- To deliver voice messages to those same people.
- To operate core features (friend requests, ride rooms, route sharing).

We do **not** sell your personal information or use it for advertising.

## Who can see your data

- **Location & voice:** only your accepted friends and members of a ride room you
  are currently in. People outside those groups cannot see your location.
- **Service providers:** we use Supabase (database, authentication, storage) and
  Mapbox (maps, search, directions) to run the app. Sign‑in is provided by Apple
  and Google.

## Data retention

- **Voice messages** are automatically removed after 24 hours, and you can delete
  your own at any time.
- **Location** is only shared while sharing is on; it stops when you turn it off.
- **Ride rooms** automatically close and are deleted after 12 hours.
- **Account data** is kept until you delete your account.

## Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account at any time in the app: **ME → Delete
Account**. This removes your profile, friends, location, voice data, and your login.

## Permissions

- **Location:** to show you and your crew on the map.
- **Microphone:** to record voice messages.

You can change these any time in your device Settings.

## Children

Crew is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your
country) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

## Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change accordingly.
